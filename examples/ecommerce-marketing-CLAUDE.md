# CLAUDE.md — D2C E-Commerce Brand

## Company Context

We are **Evergreen Goods**, a direct-to-consumer sustainable home goods brand. We sell reusable, eco-friendly alternatives to single-use household products.

- **Products:** Reusable wraps, bamboo kitchenware, organic cleaning supplies, zero-waste starter kits
- **Target audience:** Environmentally conscious consumers, ages 25-45, primarily women, urban and suburban, household income $60K+
- **Positioning:** "Sustainable living made simple." Premium quality, beautiful design, guilt-free convenience.
- **Price range:** $12-$85 per product, $120-$200 for bundles
- **Stage:** 3 years in, profitable, ~$8M annual revenue, team of 12

## Brand Personality

- **Warm and encouraging** — never preachy or guilt-tripping about sustainability
- **Aspirational but attainable** — luxury feel, accessible price
- **Knowledgeable** — we educate without lecturing
- **Visual tone:** Earthy colors, clean layouts, lifestyle photography with real homes
- **Voice examples:**
  - YES: "Small swaps, big impact. Start with one."
  - NO: "Stop destroying the planet with plastic."
  - YES: "Designed to last — and look beautiful doing it."
  - NO: "Our products are eco-friendly and sustainable."

## Key Channels

### Email (Primary revenue driver — 35% of revenue)
- Welcome series (5 emails)
- Post-purchase education sequence
- Weekly newsletter (Tuesday sends)
- Abandoned cart (3-email sequence)
- Win-back campaigns (60/90/120 day)
- VIP segment for repeat buyers (early access, exclusive bundles)

### Social Media
- **Instagram:** Primary channel. Mix of lifestyle content, UGC, product highlights, educational reels. Post 5x/week.
- **TikTok:** Educational content ("swap this for that"), behind-the-scenes, founder stories. Post 3x/week.
- **Pinterest:** Product pins, lifestyle boards, how-to guides. Drives significant organic traffic.
- **Facebook:** Community group (12K members), retargeting ads

### Paid Advertising
- **Meta Ads:** Prospecting (lookalikes of buyers), retargeting (website visitors, cart abandoners)
- **Google Ads:** Branded search, shopping ads, competitor terms
- **Pinterest Ads:** Shopping and awareness campaigns
- **Budget:** ~$40K/month, target ROAS 4x+

## Seasonal Planning

- **January:** "New Year, New Habits" campaign — starter kits, resolution bundles
- **March-April:** Spring cleaning campaign, Earth Day (major moment — plan 6 weeks ahead)
- **June:** Summer entertaining collection launch
- **September:** Back-to-school sustainable swaps, fall collection
- **November:** Black Friday/Cyber Monday (plan starts in September, "Green Friday" positioning)
- **December:** Holiday gift guides, gift bundles, last-ship-date urgency

## Important Notes

- All sustainability claims must be specific and verifiable — no greenwashing
- UGC performs 3x better than branded creative in Meta Ads
- Email revenue goal: 35-40% of total revenue
- Always include "impact stats" in campaigns (e.g., "This swap saves 200 plastic bags per year")
- Product photography is in `assets/photos/` — use lifestyle shots for social, clean shots for ads
