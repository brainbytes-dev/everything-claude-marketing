# CLAUDE.md — B2B SaaS Marketing Team

## Company Context

We are **Acme Analytics**, a B2B SaaS platform for product analytics. Our product helps product teams understand user behavior, measure feature adoption, and reduce churn.

- **Product:** Self-serve analytics platform with enterprise tier
- **Target audience:** Product managers, growth engineers, and data analysts at mid-market SaaS companies (200-2000 employees)
- **Positioning:** "The analytics platform built for product teams, not data teams." We compete on ease of use and speed to insight vs. Amplitude, Mixpanel, and Heap.
- **Pricing model:** Freemium with usage-based pricing. Free tier drives PLG motion.
- **Stage:** Series B, 150 employees, ~$20M ARR

## Brand Voice

- **Tone:** Confident but not arrogant. Expert but accessible.
- **Style:** Clear, concise, jargon-free. We explain complex concepts simply.
- **Personality:** The smart friend who makes analytics feel approachable.
- **Avoid:** Buzzwords ("synergy", "leverage", "disrupt"), hyperbole ("revolutionary", "game-changing"), and condescending language.
- **Writing rules:**
  - Active voice over passive
  - Short sentences and paragraphs
  - Use "you" and "your" — speak to the reader directly
  - Lead with the benefit, follow with the feature

## Marketing Channels

- **Blog:** Long-form thought leadership and product-led content (2-3 posts/week)
- **Email:** Nurture sequences, product updates, weekly digest
- **LinkedIn:** Primary social channel. Mix of company posts and founder personal brand.
- **Twitter/X:** Product updates, industry commentary, community engagement
- **Webinars:** Monthly with customers and industry experts
- **Docs:** Product documentation doubles as SEO content
- **Paid:** Google Ads (branded + competitor), LinkedIn Ads (ABM campaigns)
