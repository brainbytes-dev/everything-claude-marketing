# Marketing Compliance

## GDPR / DSGVO (EU)

ALWAYS comply with EU data protection rules:

- **Consent first**: Never collect, store, or process personal data without explicit, informed consent
- **Purpose limitation**: Data collected for marketing may only be used for the stated purpose
- **Right to erasure**: Honor deletion requests within 30 days
- **Data minimization**: Collect only what is strictly necessary
- **Cookie consent**: All non-essential cookies require opt-in consent (no pre-checked boxes)
- **Privacy policy**: Every landing page, form, and app must link to a privacy policy
- **Data processing agreements**: Required for all third-party tools handling EU user data
- **Breach notification**: Report data breaches to authorities within 72 hours

## CAN-SPAM (US Email)

ALL marketing emails must:
- Include a valid physical postal address
- Provide a clear, working unsubscribe mechanism
- Honor unsubscribe requests within 10 business days
- Use accurate "From" and "Reply-To" headers
- Use non-deceptive subject lines
- Clearly identify the message as an advertisement (if applicable)

## FTC Disclosure (US)

- **Sponsored content**: Clearly disclose all paid partnerships (#ad, #sponsored, "Paid partnership with...")
- **Affiliate links**: Disclose affiliate relationships before the link
- **Endorsements**: Testimonials must reflect typical consumer experience or include disclaimer
- **Influencer content**: Disclosure must be clear, conspicuous, and unavoidable (not buried in hashtags)
- **Native advertising**: Must be clearly distinguishable from editorial content
- **Reviews**: Disclose if products were received for free

## Platform-Specific Ad Policies

### Meta (Facebook/Instagram)
- No before/after images implying unrealistic results
- No personal attributes assumptions ("Are you overweight?")
- No discriminatory targeting by race, religion, sexual orientation
- Special ad categories for housing, employment, credit
- Landing page must match ad content

### Google Ads
- No misleading claims or clickbait
- Destination URL must work and match ad content
- Trademark policies — cannot use competitor trademarks in ad text (with exceptions)
- Healthcare/pharma ads require certification
- Financial services ads require compliance disclosures

### LinkedIn
- Professional content standards
- No misleading job offers
- B2B targeting restrictions for certain industries
- Sponsored InMail frequency limits

### TikTok
- Influencer content must use "Paid Partnership" label
- No misleading product claims
- Age-gating for restricted products
- Music licensing requirements for ads

## Accessibility

- Images must have alt text
- Videos should have captions/subtitles
- Color contrast must meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards
- Forms must be screen-reader compatible
- Email must be readable without images loaded

## Industry-Specific

- **Healthcare**: No unsubstantiated health claims, FDA disclaimer requirements
- **Financial**: Required risk disclosures, APR/fee transparency
- **Alcohol**: Age-gating, no targeting minors, responsible drinking messaging
- **Gambling**: Jurisdiction-specific licensing, responsible gambling messaging
- **Children**: COPPA compliance, no data collection from under-13s

## Compliance Checklist

Before publishing ANY marketing content:
- [ ] Consent mechanisms in place for data collection
- [ ] Unsubscribe/opt-out available and working
- [ ] Disclosures present for sponsored/affiliate content
- [ ] Claims substantiated with evidence
- [ ] Privacy policy linked where required
- [ ] Platform-specific policies reviewed
- [ ] Accessibility standards met
- [ ] Industry-specific regulations checked
