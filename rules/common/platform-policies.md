# Platform Policies

## Core Principle

Every platform has its own rules. Violating them can result in ad account bans, content removal, or permanent suspension. Know the rules before you post.

## Meta (Facebook & Instagram)

### Ad Policies
- No discriminatory practices in housing, employment, or credit ads
- No before/after images that imply unrealistic results
- No personal attribute assertions ("We know you're struggling with...")
- Special ad categories require broader targeting (no age, gender, ZIP exclusions)
- Landing pages must match ad content and be functional
- No misleading buttons or UI elements in ad creative
- Video ads: no deceptive play buttons or fake interactivity

### Content Policies
- No misinformation or manipulated media
- Fact-checking program may label disputed content
- Community Standards apply to organic and paid content
- Instagram branded content requires "Paid partnership" tag

### Technical Requirements
- Ad images: minimal text overlay (check with text overlay tool)
- Aspect ratios: 1:1 (feed), 9:16 (Stories/Reels), 16:9 (in-stream)
- Character limits: headline 40, primary text 125 (visible), link description 30

## Google Ads

### Ad Policies
- No misleading claims or clickbait
- Destination URL must work, be relevant, and match ad content
- No bridge pages or doorway pages
- Trademark policies restrict competitor name usage in ad text
- No circumventing Google's review systems

### Industry Restrictions
- Healthcare/pharma: certification required, no unapproved drug claims
- Financial services: required disclosures (APR, fees, risks)
- Gambling: jurisdiction-specific licensing required
- Alcohol: restricted targeting, age-gating required
- Legal services: jurisdiction-specific restrictions

### Technical Requirements
- Headline: 30 characters (up to 15 headlines in RSAs)
- Description: 90 characters (up to 4 descriptions)
- Display URL: 15 characters per path field
- No excessive capitalization, punctuation, or symbols

## LinkedIn

### Ad Policies
- Professional content standards — no casual/unprofessional content
- No misleading job offers or fake employment opportunities
- B2B targeting restrictions for certain regulated industries
- Sponsored InMail has frequency caps per member
- Document ads must contain substantial content (not just a form)

### Content Policies
- No harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory content
- Professional community guidelines apply
- Newsletter and article content must be original
- No engagement bait ("Like if you agree!")

### Technical Requirements
- Sponsored content image: 1200x627 (1.91:1)
- Headline: 70 characters (truncates on mobile at ~50)
- Introductory text: 150 characters visible
- Carousel: 2-10 cards

## TikTok

### Ad Policies
- No misleading product claims or fake demonstrations
- Influencer ads must use "Paid Partnership" disclosure
- Age-gating for restricted products (alcohol, gambling)
- Music in ads requires licensing (separate from organic)
- No unauthorized use of TikTok branding

### Content Policies
- Community guidelines govern organic content
- No dangerous challenges or harmful behavior promotion
- AI-generated content must be labeled
- No spam, fake engagement, or artificial inflation

### Technical Requirements
- Video: 9:16 vertical, 5-60 seconds
- Resolution: 720p minimum, 1080p recommended
- Text safe zone: keep important elements within center frame
- Sound-on by default — design for audio

## X (Twitter)

### Ad Policies
- Political advertising allowed with transparency requirements
- No misleading claims or deceptive practices
- Sensitive content categories have targeting restrictions
- Branded hashtags must not be misleading

### Content Policies
- Paid partnerships should be disclosed
- No coordinated inauthentic behavior
- No spam or platform manipulation

### Technical Requirements
- Tweet: 280 characters
- Image: 1600x900 (16:9) or 1080x1080 (1:1)
- Video: up to 2:20, 1080p recommended
- Threads: no character limit per tweet in thread

## YouTube

### Ad Policies
- Skippable in-stream: 12 seconds minimum, skip after 5
- Non-skippable: 15-20 seconds maximum
- No misleading thumbnails or titles
- Must comply with YouTube's advertiser-friendly guidelines

### Content Policies
- Community Guidelines and Terms of Service apply
- Sponsorship disclosures required (in-video and description)
- AI-generated content must be labeled
- Made for Kids content has restricted features

### Technical Requirements
- Thumbnail: 1280x720 (16:9)
- Title: 100 characters (60 visible in search)
- Description: 5000 characters

## General Best Practices

1. **Review platform policies before every campaign launch** — they change frequently
2. **Keep records** of ad approvals and policy compliance checks
3. **Have a contingency plan** for ad disapprovals or account restrictions
4. **Stay updated** on policy changes through official platform blogs and newsletters
5. **Don't assume** cross-platform consistency — each platform has unique rules
6. **Appeal process** — know how to appeal rejected ads on each platform
7. **Account health** — monitor account standing and quality scores regularly
